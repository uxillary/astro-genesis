metadata

id – a unique identifier (e.g. exp_001)
title – name of the experiment
authors – list of author names
year – publication or experiment year
organism – what living thing or material was studied
experiment_type – type or field of study (e.g. Microgravity Botany)
platform – where it happened (ISS, Shuttle, etc.)
keywords – short list of key topics
sections – main text parts:
abstract
methods
results
conclusion
links – URLs to NASA data or publications

summary – short AI-generated overview
graph / metrics – data for charts (year trends, related studies, etc.) - if necessary for that specific publication
